Spirometry is a key medical test for assessing lung function. It measures how much air you can inhale, exhale, and how quickly you can do so. This test helps diagnose and monitor conditions like asthma, COPD, and other lung diseases. With its simplicity and accuracy, spirometry is essential in guiding treatment and improving respiratory health.
